SUPPLY AGREEMENT
     This Supply Agreement (the “Agreement”), is made and entered into as of the last date of execution appearing on the signature page hereto (the “Effective Date”), by and between Trigon Incorporated, a Pennsylvania corporation, having its principal place of business in McMurray, Pennsylvania and which operates the business division it calls “Stelkast” (“STELKAST”) and MAKO Surgical Corp., a Delaware corporation, having its principal place of business in Hollywood, Florida (“MAKO”). STELKAST and MAKO are sometimes referred to herein individually as a “Party” and collectively as the “Parties.”
Recitals
     WHEREAS, STELKAST has developed a certain “Implant” (as defined below);
     WHEREAS, STELKAST and MAKO entered into that certain Binding Letter of Intent, dated April 27, 2005 in which STELKAST agreed to grant to MAKO a non-exclusive, worldwide license to certain rights relating to the Implant (the “LOI”); and
